Welcome to Sentinels Republic
Setup Complete

While your game downloads, here are some tips and tricks to help you get started on Sentinels Republic.

Account Creation

Don't forget to create a forum and game account. Make sure you are logged into your forum account when creating a game account, then use your Game Account information to sign into the game.

Community

Galaxy Chat: You can chat with other players in-game no matter where you are. On your chat box, select the "SR Galaxy" tab.

Auction Chat: Check the "Auction" channel in game to see where players are buffing and selling their goods. Right click a chat tab and add or modify a chat tab to include the "Auction" channel.

SWG Options - Read online

Use the "SWG Options" button in the launcher to configure your game.

Graphics: Set your Game Resolution to match your monitor's resolution, then set Vertex/Pixel Shader to "2.0 (override)"" and check "Use Safe Renderer". It is also recommended to run the game in both "Windowed Mode" and "Borderless Window" mode.

Sound: Make sure "Disable Audio" is NOT selected.

Game: Check "Skip Intro Sequence" and if your computer performs well, "Disabled Character System LoD Manager."

Advanced: Check "Disable World Preloading" and "Disabled File Caching."

Game Play Suggestions

When you start on SR, after first creating your character, speak with the Buff Provider to learn about the server and get a set of free starter buffs. You will also be supplied with a BARC, starter weapons and starter armor. Don't forget to migrate your stats before leaving the station!

There is a 12 hour timer on creating and deleting characters. If you create or delete a character, you will not be able to create a new one for 12 hours.

Buff NPCs are located in Theed, Coronet and Mos Eisley Med Center and Cantinas, but for a price and short duration. It is highly recommend you check the auction channel or ask in galaxy chat to see who is buffing.

If you are playing as an Artisan, you can visit the Tansarii Courier on Tansarii Station (accessible from any Starport) and trade random craftables in for credits.
